## 1. Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how nhdiscord.com (“we,” “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website or associated Discord services.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide  
- Profile details  
- Optional bio or preferences  
- Dating information (18+ only)  
- Safety or moderation reports  

### 2.2 Information from Discord OAuth  
When you sign in via OAuth, we may receive:
- Discord username  
- Discord ID  
- Avatar  
- Server membership status  
- OAuth scopes explicitly granted  


### 2.3 Automatically Collected Data  
- IP address  
- Browser type  
- Timestamps  
- Basic usage analytics  

Used for security and functionality.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
- Authenticate via Discord  
- Operate website features  
- Provide adult-only matching (18+)  
- Enforce moderation and safety  
- Prevent fraud and misuse  
- Comply with legal obligations  

We do **not** sell or license your data.

---

## 4. Minors’ Information

### 4.1 Restrictions  
Minors (13–17) may only access non-dating features.  
We do not collect dating or romantic info from minors.

### 4.2 Safety  
Reports involving minors receive highest priority.  
We minimize stored data relating to minors.

---

## 5. Data Sharing
We may share limited data with:
- Discord (for authentication)  
- Moderation staff  
- Law enforcement when legally required  

We do not sell or rent data.

---

## 6. Data Retention
Data is retained only as long as needed for:
- Platform operation  
- Moderation  
- Security  
- Legal compliance  

Users may request deletion where applicable.

---

## 7. Security Measures
We implement safeguards including:
- Encrypted connections  
- Limited-access data storage  
- Administrative protections  

No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps.

---

## 8. User Rights
You may request:
- Access to your data  
- Correction of inaccurate data  
- Deletion of stored data  
- Revocation of OAuth permissions  

---

## 9. Cookies & Tracking
We may use cookies or session tokens for:
- Login sessions  
- Security  
- Basic website functionality  

We do not use invasive tracking.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Policy.  
Continued use after changes means acceptance.
